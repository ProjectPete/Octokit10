About CloudCrusader

CloudCrusader is the alter ego of Christophe Lams, full time Azure Cloud Solution Architect @ Synergics in collaboration with Microsoft Belgium providing Technical Presales and training for ISV's. Occasionally, he also travels the world as Azure trainer for the American learning partner Opsgility.
