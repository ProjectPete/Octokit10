Introduction

Maruthachalam is a SharePoint Consultant in a leading software company and have expertise in SharePoint Online, SharePoint 2013, SharePoint Migration, SSRS Integration with SharePoint, Power BI and it's integration with SharePoint, MSBI, and MCTS in SharePoint 2010.
Maruthachalam has experience in implementing SharePoint solutions in various domains such as Consumer Durable, Health Care, Aerospace and Oil & Gas.
He was first awarded Microsoft TechNet Technical Guru Award in 2015 and followed in 2017 as well.
